Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Tintora Sort handles information in connection with the use of the game.

1. Introduction

Tintora Sort is a mobile puzzle game. The game does not require players to create an account. Certain information may nevertheless be processed automatically when the game is used, including locally stored gameplay data and information handled by device, platform, and advertising services.

2. Information Collected

Depending on how the game is used, the following categories of information may be processed:

  • Local gameplay and settings data, such as progress, hearts, timers, audio settings, vibration settings, and in-game state stored on the device.
  • Internet and device information, including technical request information needed for connectivity checks and operation of online features.
  • Advertising-related information processed by advertising partners, which may include device identifiers, IP address, approximate location derived from IP, ad interaction data, and device or app information.
  • Information provided by email, if you contact us directly.

3. How Information Is Used

Information may be used to operate the game, maintain saved progress and settings, verify internet connectivity, display and manage advertisements, respond to communications, detect technical issues, and protect the integrity and security of the game.

4. Advertising and Third-Party Services

The game may use third-party advertising and related service providers. Those providers may process information in accordance with their own terms and privacy practices in order to serve ads, measure performance, prevent fraud, and comply with legal obligations.

5. Local Storage and Retention

Gameplay progress and settings may be stored locally on the device. Local data generally remains on the device until it is removed by the player, the app data is cleared, or the game is uninstalled. Information handled by third-party providers is subject to their own retention periods and policies.

6. Children’s Privacy

The game does not use an in-game account system. If you believe that personal information relating to a child has been provided to us directly, please contact us so that the matter can be reviewed.

7. Your Choices

  • You may stop using the game at any time.
  • You may remove locally stored data through your device settings, by clearing app data, or by uninstalling the game.
  • You may contact us with questions about this Privacy Policy.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updated version will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.

9. Contact
